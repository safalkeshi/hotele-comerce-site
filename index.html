The Blog

Welcome to Blossom Hotel, your serene retreat nestled in the lush greenery of Godawari, Lalitpur. Surrounded by the natural beauty of the Himalayas, Blossom Hotel offers a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, making it the perfect destination for travelers seeking tranquility and comfort.Our hotel, which is conveniently close to Kathmandu, is the perfect place to discover the culture and comfort. At Blossom Hotel, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional hospitality and personalized service to each of our guests. Our boutique hotel features elegantly designed rooms, blending modern amenities with the warmth of traditional Nepali architecture. Whether you’re here for a weekend getaway, a business trip, or a family vacation, we strive to create a memorable experience tailored to your needs. Located just a short drive from Kathmandu, our hotel is ideally situated for exploring the cultural and natural attractions of the region. From the famous Godawari Botanical Garden to the ancient temples and hiking trails, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. At Blossom Hotel, we believe in sustainability and have implemented eco-friendly practices to preserve the pristine environment around us. Our on-site restaurant serves delicious local and international cuisine, prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. We invite you to unwind in our tranquil gardens, take in the breathtaking mountain views, and experience the warmth and hospitality that Blossom Hotel is known for. Whether you’re seeking relaxation, adventure, or a bit of both, Blossom Hotel is your home away from home in the heart of Godawari.
